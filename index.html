Hello! I am Yinan Zheng. I am a 2nd-year PhD student at AIR, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xianyuan Zhan, Prof. Shengbo Eben Li and Prof. Jingjing Liu.

My research focuses on advancing AI-powered solutions for safe and superhuman-level real-world decision-making and LLMs. Currently, I am working on:

  • (SafeAI+X) Ensuring the reliability of learning-based decision-making systems and the alignment of LLMs in safety-critical applications.
  • (GenAI+X) Leveraging the power of generative models to enhance planning, control in robotics and autonomous driving.
  • (IL/RL+X) Advancing IL/RL for scalable, efficient, and superhuman performance in decision-making and LLMs.
I am open to collaboration, feel free to reach me out!
